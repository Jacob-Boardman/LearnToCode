Egg Drop soup

Prep: 5min

Cook Time: 15min

Serves 2

Ingredients Amount Notes
Egg 3 large
Chicken/Vegtable Stock 500ml Prefereably unsalted, but this could be from a stock cube for conveinence
Star Aniseed 1 pod
Corriander Seeds A few seeds
Spring onioin Enough for a garnish on top Finely sliced
Roasted seaseme seed oil 1tsp
Light Soy Sauce 1tbsp Add less if your stock has salt in it and is mainly for taste
  1. Heat up the stock or melt stock cube in boilng water (500ml) then redue to a simmer over a medium low heat.
  2. Once the stock is simmering. take the Star Aniseed and corriander seeds and add them to a little cheese cloth and add tie with kitchen twine or add to a fine tea streainer and add to stock. Simmer this spices for 10 minuets
  3. Whilst thats simmering add the 3 eggs to a bowl and whisk heavily till they are consistently mixed together.

Breakfast Bagels

Prep: 3min

Cook Time: 10-15min

Serves 1

Ingredients Amount Notes
Bagels 1 standard This can be any of your choice
Mushrooms 50g
Egg 2 large
Bacon 4 thin rashors Ideally two thin pieces per half bagel
Suace To suit
  1. Let's start off by preparing our food. First of cut you bagel in half length ways so that you have two circular halfs. Then slice the mushrooms roughly.
  2. Next add some oil to a frying pan on and put on a medium heat.
  3. Add your bacon to the pan and leave it alone to cook on the first side for about 8 minuets, then flip them over and cook for the same time. After you have flipped the bacon add the mushrooms into the same pan and start frying them off.
  4. When you have about 3 minuets left for the bacon move them tothe side of the frying pan with your mushrooms. Next crack both eggs into the other side of the frying pan and let them start to cook. At this point you can now put your bagel halfs into the toaster.
  5. Watch you bacon mushrooms and eggs and turn off the heat when you are happy with how they are cooked.
  6. Assembling is the easiest part. Take your toasted bagles halfs add themto your plate and apread butter (if prefered), at a little bit of suace and spread evenly. Take the bacon out first and add two pieces to each half. Lay your egg on top next. Then finish with your fried mushrooms.
  7. Dig in and Enjoy!
