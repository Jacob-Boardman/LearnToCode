Process

Here will give an idea of the process of how the recipes are going to be done.

They will be given within an ordered list of instrsctions like so:

  1. Mix the Flour, Sugar, and Eggs into a bowl
  2. Add into a baking tray
  3. Cook in oven for 20 minuets

There will also be a table to suite showing you the ingredients and the measurements that are needed, an example is shown below

Ingredients needed
