Breakfast

Breakfast Sandwich

Prep: 5min

Cook Time: 15min

Serves 4

Ingredients Amount Notes
Egg 6 large
Butter 4 tsp Preferably usalted, and a little extra for toasting
Crème Fraîche 1 tbsp
Salt and pepper To taste
Chives 1 bunch (handful) Finely chopped
Sandwitch Buns 4 Which ever buns you prefer
Garlic Mayo To your desire
Bacon 8 Rashors Normally 2 each but you can do more or less
Cheese 8 slices/50g shredded This is freely up to you
  1. In a medium saucepan, bring 1 inch (2.5cm) of water to a gentle simmer over medium heat Place a heatproof bowl on top of the pot making sure the bowl does not touch the water.
  2. Crack the eggs into the bowl, along with 2 tablespoons (28g) butter. Cook, constantly whisking, for 8 to 10 minutes, or until the eggs are cooked and starting to finely curd and come together. Remove from the heat while still creamy and soft.
  3. Whisk in the remaining 2 tablespoons (28g) butter, along with the crème fraîche, until melted and well distributed. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Fold in the chives.
  4. Toast the buns in a pan with butter until golden brown on the cut sides. To assemble each sandwich, spread garlic mayo on the bottom buns. Then add 2 slices of bacon, followed by a generous mound of scrambled egg, and finally 2 slices of cheese. Torch or broil to melt the cheese. Top with your other half of the bun, and enjoy immediately.
